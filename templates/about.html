About Us

Building a Sustainable Future Through Education

Green Campus Initiative was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate environmental science students at the National University of Uzbekistan. What started as a small tree-planting campaign has grown into a nationwide movement transforming how universities approach environmental sustainability.

Our mission is to create a culture of environmental responsibility within higher education institutions. We believe that universities are not just places of learning - they are communities that can model sustainable practices and inspire the next generation of environmental leaders.

Today, we work with over 47 universities across Uzbekistan, engaging more than 12,000 active volunteers in projects ranging from campus greening and waste management to renewable energy initiatives and environmental education programs.

Our approach combines scientific research with practical action. We develop evidence-based solutions tailored to each campus's unique needs, ensuring that our initiatives create lasting impact rather than temporary improvements.
