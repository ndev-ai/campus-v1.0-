Our Mission

Creating a Culture of Sustainable Development

Our mission goes beyond greening campus territories. We aim to cultivate environmental consciousness among future leaders, embedding sustainability into the core of higher education.

We believe that universities are the birthplace of ideas that shape society. By transforming campuses into models of environmental responsibility, we inspire students to become agents of change in their communities and careers.

Our Journey

From Idea to National Movement

Key milestones in our mission to transform Uzbekistan's campuses

2018

The Beginning

Green Campus Initiative was founded by a group of environmental science students at National University of Uzbekistan with a vision to make campuses greener.

2019

First Major Project

Launched our first campus-wide recycling program and planted 1,000 trees across 5 university campuses in Tashkent.

2020

National Recognition

Received government support and expanded to 15 universities across Uzbekistan. Launched online environmental education programs.

2022

International Partnerships

Partnered with international environmental organizations and expanded our programs to include renewable energy initiatives.

2024

Award & Growth

Won "Green Campus of the Year" award. Now active in 47 universities with over 12,000 volunteers nationwide.

Our Vision

A Greener Tomorrow Starts Today

We envision a future where every university in Uzbekistan serves as a beacon of environmental responsibility - where sustainability is not just taught in classrooms but practiced in every corner of the campus.

Our goal for 2030 is to partner with 100 universities, train 50,000 environmental leaders, and reduce campus carbon footprints by 40% across our network.
